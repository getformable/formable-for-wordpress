Integrates Formable into your WordPress install. This allows you to add your Formable form in WordPress.

== Description ==
The main purpose of this plugin is to connect your WordPress install with [Formable](https://www.getformable.com).

You'll give WordPress permission to read your account's data and start including Formable forms in your posts and pages.

== Installation ==
1. Upload "formable" folder to the "/wp-content/plugins/" directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the "Plugins" menu in WordPress.
1. Connect to your Formable account on the "Formable" menu item.
5. Give your WordPress install permission to read your account information.
6. Party!
